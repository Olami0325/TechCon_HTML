History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local event for technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform that attracts thousands of participants and influential speakers from around the world.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and learning within the global tech community. TechCon empowers developers, entrepreneurs, and students to shape the future of technology.

Past Speakers

Here are some of the outstanding speakers who have graced the TechCon stage:

Karuana Gatimu is a business architect and productivity expert currently working in the M365 Customer Advocacy in Collaborative Apps and Platform group. She also is the co-host of the CloudDev Clarity show and Browser Native. She's also has been awarded the Microsoft MVP for Office Apps and Services since 2017 and is a core member of the Microsoft 365 PnP Team.
